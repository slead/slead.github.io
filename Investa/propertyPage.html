135 King Street is located in the heart of Sydney’s CBD, surrounded by high-end retail stores and global brands on Castlereagh Street and adjacent Pitt Street Mall.
Quick undercover access takes you directly to trains at both Town Hall and Martin Place stations, with major bus routes on the doorstep and new CBD cycleways making access even easier to 135 King Street. The building incorporates international retail brands, H&M and Zara Home below 23 levels of office space. The 27,252sqm of A-grade office space offers 1,200sqm floor plates and a 5-star NABERS Energy rating. 135 King offers tenants high quality office space, upgraded services services and and new lobby cafe and End-of-Trip amenities.
